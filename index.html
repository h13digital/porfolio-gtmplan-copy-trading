PART VI: RISK MANAGEMENT (CRYPTO SPECIFIC)

Risk Assessment

Scenario 1: Exchange API Restrictions

Why: Binance/Bybit restrict copy trading APIs

Action Plan:

  • Diversify to KuCoin, OKX, Gate.io APIs
  • Develop manual copy trading features
  • Focus on educational content during transition

Scenario 2: Bear Market Sentiment

Why: Crypto downturn reduces trading activity

Action Plan:

  • Pivot to educational content and community building
  • Introduce spot copy trading (lower risk)
  • Focus on DCA and long-term strategies

Scenario 3: Regulatory Challenges

Why: Country bans copy trading platforms

Action Plan:

  • Geo-block restricted regions
  • Focus on crypto-friendly jurisdictions
  • Legal structure in Singapore/Dubai
